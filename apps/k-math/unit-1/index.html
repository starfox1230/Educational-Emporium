What Your Star Will Learn in Unit 1:

  • Count and recognize shiny quantities from 0 to 10.
  • Read and write the magical numerals from 0 to 10.
  • Identify sparkling nickels and pennies and find their value up to 10 cents.
  • Discover delightful combinations that make 10 (like 6 and 4, or 7 and 3)!
  • Find cool combinations that make 5.
